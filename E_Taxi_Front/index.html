How to Order a Luxury Taxi

Making your travel luxurious and convenient is just a few steps away. Follow these simple instructions to order a taxi with us.

  • Search about GrandCruize Webapp in any kind of browser.
  • Create an account and log in using your credentials.
  • Enter your current location and destination in the app.
  • Choose your preferred vehicle class and any additional services you may need.
  • Review the fare estimate and confirm your booking.

Congratulations! Your luxury taxi is now booked. Our professional chauffeur will pick you up promptly, ensuring a comfortable and stylish journey.
